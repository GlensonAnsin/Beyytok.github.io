My Personal Skills

“When it comes to mastering a skill, time is the magic ingredient.”

Organizing
I tend to like it when things are organized. For instance, I organize my things in terms of its functions, pictures on my phone in terms of events, medical documents, school documents, and many more. Even in my laptop, I have different files dedicated to every subject and each file consists of another file where I put all activity sheets if ever there are any. Though my way of organizing things may be tiring, but in the end, it is very convenient for me especially when I want to find, study or access something.

C++ Programming
I may not be a pro in programming yet, but C++ programming is being taught to us as it is a part of our major subject. When coding, we have to think of other alternatives or other possible situations on what users would input on the program. This would enable us to think creatively which could be hard at times, but very satisfying when we get what we want for the program we made.

HTML & CSS
I was always curious as to how professionals in the programming field create a website. This is because I was once curious, wanted to make my own website for fun but didn't know how. Now as we are taught how to create a website while also exploring on our own, I find it tiring yet satisying in the end when I get the design that I want. Now I undestand why Web Developers have a huge amound of salary. It's because it takes a lot of time and effort in order to create one.

Collaboration
Whenever we are given the task to perform it with a group, I try my best to contribute in order for us to get good results in our efforts. This is done through sharing different ideas to my groupmates, at the same time being open minded and listen to them too. In addition, to have a really effective discussions between us, we must also be open to critizations in order to learn and grow as a group.

Diplomacy/Empathy
Knowing myself, I tend to care about others a lot. Having this skill enables me to be compassionate and empathize other people's thoughts and feelings. This is because I care about relationships with my family, friends, and even other people and how I should manage them. Acquiring this skill would also allow me to choose my words wisely especially in bad situations. Sometimes it may be hard because I am not that good in communicating but I have to do something that could be talking or other than talking so I can manage my relationship with people.

Self-Motivation
Life isn't always about rainbows and sunshine, smiles and laughters. There are also times where we experience the opposite, which is okay. What matters for me is how I deal with it. Self-motivation is a skill of mine that I have recently discovered. In most situations, it made my loud, overthinking mind quiet and at peace. This skill will help me to become mentally and emotionally strong, at the same time, it enables me to acknowledge my pain but would use it as a tool to keep myself together and a reason for me to keep going in life.
